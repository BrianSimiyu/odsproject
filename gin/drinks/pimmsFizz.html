Pimm's Fizz

Ingridients

  • 1 ounce Beefeater gin
  • 1 ounce Pimm's No. 1 liqueur
  • 3/4 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce grenadine
  • 1/4 ounce ginger syrup
  • 1 egg white
  • Soda water, to top
  • 2-3 cranberries

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a shaker and vigorously dry-shake (without ice).
  2. Add ice and shake again until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a Collins glass over fresh ice.
  4. Top with soda water.
  5. Garnish with 2 or 3 fresh cranberries.
