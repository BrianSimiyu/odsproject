Cotter Swizzle

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 ounces Ransom Old Tom gin
  • 1 ounce ginger liqueur
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 ounce red wine vinegar*
  • 4 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Garnish: sliced ginger

Steps

  1. Add the gin, liqueur, lime juice and red wine into a Collins glass with crushed ice and swizzle until the ice starts to melt.
  2. Top with more crushed ice to mound and gently pat down.
  3. Add the Angostura bitters over the top of the ice.
  4. Garnish with thinly sliced ginger on top of the ice.
