Unsafe at Any Speed

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces Appleton Reserve Jamaican rum
  • 3/4 ounce sambuca
  • 1/2 ounce St. George spiced pear liqueur
  • 1/4 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce Coco Lopez cream of coconut
  • 1/4 teaspoon Rare Tea Cellars matcha powder
  • 3 ounces Topo Chico sparkling mineral water, chilled

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients except the sparkling water into a shaker and vigorously dry-shake (without ice).
  2. Add ice and shake again until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a Collins glass over fresh ice.
  4. Slowly pour a stream of the sparkling water into the center of the glass until the foam rises slightly above the rim of the glass.
