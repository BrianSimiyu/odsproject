Don Quixote

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces Don Q Cristal rum
  • 1 ounce mango-pandan syrup*
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce Appleton Estate 12 Year rum
  • Garnish: dehydrated lime wheel

Steps

  1. Add the Don Q rum, mango-pandan syrup and lime juice into a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a rocks glass over crushed ice.
  3. Float the Appleton Estate rum on top.
  4. Garnish with the dehydrated lime wheel.
