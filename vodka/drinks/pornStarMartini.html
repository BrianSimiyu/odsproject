Porn Star Martini

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces vanilla-flavored vodka
  • 1/2 ounce passion fruit liqueur (such as Passo√£ or De Kuyper)
  • 1 ounce passion fruit puree
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce vanilla simple syrup
  • 2 ounces sparkling wine, chilled
  • Garnish: 1/2 passion fruit

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients except sparkling wine into a shaker with ice and shake vigorously until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
  3. Garnish with a passion fruit half.
  4. Serve with a sparkling wine sidecar (on the side).
